Alpha Micro Interaction Explanation

+ Raid (8-man) Armor Sets

Link: https://xivstyle.com/
Triggers: User hovers mouse cursor over drop down text.
Rules: When the user hovers their mouse over the text, the text will highlight a color. When the user's mouse leaves the area of the text, then the text will no longer be highlighted.
Feedback: The text will change colors to indicate a highlight.
Loops and Modes: No loops or modes present in this interaction.
